Ing0t

Ing0t is a single-player-competitive-factory-building-resource-management game. You are the Cryomining Commander of the Crimson Crown Corporation a ruthless monopoly that spawns the galaxy. Your job is to create complex mining operations on top of dangerous, unstable asteroids with cryovolcanic vents. You have limited time to extract and refine the rare Cry0re, load it into rockets, and send it to space. After selling the Cry0re, the rocket returns with new raw materials, allowing you to repair and expand the operation. Adapt and optimize in order to thrive in challenging settings.
